Breakup Story!

I still remember all your words, but I prayed for you to say, "It's just a prank baby! How will I leave you my love?! ...

I still remember all your words, but I end up asking myself where did it went wrong? I gave all my time for you, you didn't give me even a minute, to ask, "why are you leaving me?" You left all of a sudden, pushing me into complete darkness. All my dreams with you was shattered, I badly wanted you next to me. I prayed for you to say, "It's just a prank baby! How will I leave you my love?!" Neither I didn't know all your love will turn for me in agony one day. The pain I went through was hard. I couldn't put up into words. Those blank DP's have seen me crying, you didnt come back to wipe those tears that longed for you. My tears rolled down my cheek, by each passing day, you never rolled back to check whether I'm alive or not. You always told me we are friends before lovers. How did that friend moved on just like that? I lost my 2am friend. Many night's have seen me struggling to let my night pass without you. I know you'll never come back. When I try to process this hard reality, a three minutes song, randomly played somewhere, takes me three years down again. Where we laughed and blushed, singing it together. Again and again, each of those memories hits me up. I still love you says my broken heart, knowing it can't be fixed!

Proposal to live in my heart

Will you accept my feelings for you? ♥️

My beating heart unknowingly thinks only of you. Then how can I stop reminiscing about you? When I wanted to forget you, suddenly so familiar you stood in front of me, still as beautiful as ever. Even if you don't feel as I feel for you, I'll keep the place by my side empty for you. I don't know how to tell you, how I feel about you, but I want you to know my heart, yet you look at the other direction. Your heart plays tuneless tunes like a wet blanket. Try putting yourself in my shoes atleast once. Maybe then you'll get to know how much I long for your warmth. You are the Sun to my Earth. It's so natural that the Earth circles around the Sun. I'm your home. I'll exist filling distance with memories, no matter when you come home. All my pains disappear all of a sudden and I feel light when I see your smile. Will you accept my feelings for you? ♥️

Story after Breakup

we broke up, but we still talk
We cannot play where it was paused, but atleast those blank DP's has got a life. struck between friends and lovers those past with your present, still makes me ...

At times it sucks When the moments of past flashes, coincides the present. The moments we laughed together, doesn't give the same feel anymore. There were day's when I longed to hear your voice, atleast like an unknown random call. But now you are a text away, this relieves me a bit! We cannot play where it was paused, but atleast those blank DP's has got a life now. There are people who judge our relationship, but we knew we are struck between friends and lovers. It's not friendship, also not a relationship. I can't call you as "mine" anymore, still I'll be there for you, in whatever walk of your life. I always care for you. Whenever you need someone to talk to, I'll be in your side. Somehow I can lie to myself about not loving you, but some part of me wishes to hear it from you, atleast for a last time or in a just kidding way. I know we have no future together. But, those past with your present, still makes me go crazy.
